Occupied Mode

This unit is equipped with a constant speed supply fan, chilled water-cooling coil, and hot water heating coil. It provides temperature control for the basement holding area. While the fan operates at a constant volume, it is provided with a variable-frequency drive to aid in balancing and to provide soft starting.

Morning Warmup

This sequence calls for a morning warm up.

Unoccupied Mode

All Air Handlers will shut down.
