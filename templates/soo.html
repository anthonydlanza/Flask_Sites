  1. MODES OF OPERATION – 24 HOUR OPERATION
    1. DISABLED MODE – SYSTEM IS PLACED IN THIS MODE ONLY WHEN COMMANDED BY OPERATOR OVERRIDE.
    2. UNIT RUNS 24 HOURS A DAY, EVERY DAY OF THE YEAR.
    3. OCCUPIED MODE – INITIALLY PROGRAM THE SYSTEM TO START OCCUPANCY AT 7 AM AND END OCCUPIED MODE AT 6PM.
    4. UNOCCUPIED MODE – SYSTEM REMAINS RUNNING WHEN NOT IN OCCUPIED MODE AT A REDUCED AIRFLOW DEMAND. ALL DOWNSTREAM TERMINAL CONTROLLERS SHALL BE PLACED IN NIGHT MODE.
    5. SAFETY SHUTDOWN MODES
      1. SEE SAFETY SHUTDOWNS SECTION OF THIS SEQUENCE FOR SAFETY SHUTDOWN CONDITIONS AND MODES.
  1. MODES OF OPERATION – 24 HOUR OPERATION
    1. DISABLED MODE – SYSTEM IS PLACED IN THIS MODE ONLY WHEN COMMANDED BY OPERATOR OVERRIDE.
    2. UNIT RUNS 24 HOURS A DAY, EVERY DAY OF THE YEAR.
    3. UNOCCUPIED MODE – SYSTEM REMAINS RUNNING WHEN NOT IN OCCUPIED MODE AT A REDUCED AIRFLOW DEMAND EXCEPT WHEN OCCUPIED CONDITIONS ARE MET.
    4. OCCUPIED MODE – SYSTEM RUNS IN THIS MODE BASED ON THE FOLLOWING CONDITIONS:
      1. ROOM OCCUPANCY SENSORS INDICATE AN OCCUPIED ROOM FOR 60 SECONDS. THE OCCUPANCY PERIOD WILL END ONCE NO MOTION HAS BEEN SENSED FOR 30 MINUTES.
      2. ROOM SENSOR OCCUPANCY OVERRIDE BUTTON IS PUSHED. THE OCCUPANCY PERIOD WILL END 2 HOURS AFTER THE BUTTON WAS LAST PUSHED.
    5. SAFETY SHUTDOWN MODES
      1. SEE SAFETY SHUTDOWNS SECTION OF THIS SEQUENCE FOR SAFETY SHUTDOWN CONDITIONS AND MODES.
